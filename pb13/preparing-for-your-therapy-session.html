Frequently Asked Questions

What conditions can red light therapy treat?

Red light therapy is effective for treating a wide range of conditions, including chronic pain, arthritis, acne, wrinkles, hair loss, tendinopathy, and inflammation. It is also used for improving skin texture, reducing scars, and supporting muscle recovery.

Does red light therapy work for hair loss?

Red light therapy is a promising treatment for hair loss, including alopecia and male pattern baldness. It improves blood flow to the scalp and stimulates hair follicles, promoting natural hair growth and improving density.
