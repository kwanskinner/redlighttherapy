Frequently Asked Questions

What is photobiomodulation in red light therapy?

Photobiomodulation refers to the process by which red and near-infrared light stimulate the mitochondria to produce more energy, enhancing cell repair and reducing inflammation. It is the foundation of how red light therapy works.

What are the safety considerations and potential side effects of red light therapy?

Red light therapy is generally safe when performed correctly. Side effects are rare but can include mild redness or sensitivity in individuals with certain skin conditions. Consulting a professional ensures the appropriate dosage and safe application.
