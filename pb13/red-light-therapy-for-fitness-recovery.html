Frequently Asked Questions

How effective is red light therapy for treating hair loss and promoting hair regrowth?

Red light therapy stimulates hair follicles, enhances blood circulation to the scalp, and can promote hair regrowth in conditions like male pattern baldness and alopecia. It complements other treatments such as minoxidil for better results.

How does red light therapy improve skin health?

Red light therapy enhances skin health by stimulating collagen production, reducing inflammation, and improving elasticity. It addresses acne, wrinkles, and scars, leaving the skin smoother and more rejuvenated.

Is red light therapy safe?

Red light therapy is considered safe when administered correctly. Side effects are rare but may include temporary skin sensitivity. Consulting a trained professional ensures that the therapy is applied appropriately for your needs.
